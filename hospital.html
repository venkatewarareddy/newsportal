      Frequently Asked Questions

      If I am staying at a non-Fraser World participating property, do I still get to take part in the draws?

      Yes, Fraser World members staying in any Frasers Hospitality properties round the world can still take part and win in the monthly and annual draws.

      Why am I not able to book the promotion at my selected property?

      Black-out dates for the offer and a minimum length of stay applies for certain Frasers Hospitality properties.

      Why do I not see certain properties in the promotion?

      We have some properties catering specifically for extended stays of over a month whereby the promotion cannot be applied.

      Do I have to book the anniversary offer in order to take part in the monthly giveaway?

      Regardless of offer booked, Fraser World members who have completed a 3-night stay in any Frasers Hospitality property between 31 March 2023 to 31 March 2024 can take part in the contest.
